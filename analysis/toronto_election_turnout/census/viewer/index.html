Geography notes
  • DA is the finest layer: 3,743 Toronto areas compared with 622 CTs and 279 ADAs.
  • In Toronto, Statistics Canada builds ADAs from CTs. DAs nest within CTs, producing a DA → CT → ADA hierarchy.
  • Citizen counts are 25% Census Profile estimates; all-resident age counts are 100% Census age-table values.
  • The citizenship metric has 16 suppressed DAs; the all-resident age metric has 9. These are different Census variables.
  • Blank DA values are official confidentiality suppressions, not zero population or unrecorded census responses.
  • CT statistics use the 585 CTs linked to Toronto DAs. Boundary-intersecting CT slivers outside that universe remain visible but are excluded from totals.
