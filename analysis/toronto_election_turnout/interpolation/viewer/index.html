Method and integrity
  • All official party and candidate totals match globally and by ward/riding.
  • No-geometry vote records are allocated using district-to-CT citizen-18+ weights.
  • Two CT citizen counts are suppressed; their citizen-rate value remains unavailable.
  • Ontario and Canada turnout rates are contextual comparisons, not Toronto validation targets.
  • Party and municipal candidate maps use vote share rather than raw counts so differently sized CTs remain comparable.
